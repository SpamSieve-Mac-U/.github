## 💡 Overview

<a href="#spamSieve">SpamSieve</a> is a dedicated spam filtering application for Mac that has earned a reputation among email power users for catching spam that built-in email client filters miss, while maintaining an exceptionally low false positive rate that prevents legitimate email from being misclassified.

The <a href="#spamSieve">Bayesian self-training filter</a> is SpamSieve's core technology — rather than relying on static rules or blocklists that spammers easily circumvent, SpamSieve builds a statistical model of what spam and legitimate email look like specifically for each user's actual email patterns. This self-training approach means SpamSieve's accuracy improves continuously as it processes more email and receives corrections, adapting to evolving spam tactics and to the specific characteristics of each user's legitimate email correspondence in a way that generic rule-based filters cannot match. The <a href="#spamSieve">deep email client integration</a> works directly within Apple Mail, Outlook, Airmail, and other popular Mac email clients — rather than operating as a separate application that requires switching contexts, SpamSieve filters mail directly within the existing email workflow, moving spam to a designated folder automatically as messages arrive.

The <a href="#spamSieve">low false positive design philosophy</a> prioritizes never losing legitimate email above all other considerations — SpamSieve's filtering algorithm and default configuration are tuned specifically to minimize the risk of misclassifying important email as spam, recognizing that a missed spam message is a minor annoyance while a misclassified legitimate email can have serious consequences. The <a href="#spamSieve">correction-based learning</a> improves the filter immediately when users mark a message as spam or not spam — every correction directly refines the statistical model, making SpamSieve's accuracy a continuously improving reflection of the specific user's actual email patterns rather than a fixed algorithm.</p>

<a href="#spamSieve">Multiple account support</a> filters spam across multiple email accounts and email clients simultaneously — a single SpamSieve installation protects all configured email accounts without requiring separate filtering configuration for each one. <a href="#spamSieve">Whitelist and blacklist management</a> allows manual override of automatic classification for specific senders — ensuring trusted senders are never filtered and known spam sources are always blocked regardless of the statistical model's assessment.

<a href="#spamSieve">Remote training capability</a> lets the filter be trained even when reviewing email remotely or from a different device — corrections made anywhere sync back to refine the central filtering model. <a href="#spamSieve">Detailed spam reporting</a> provides visibility into why specific messages were classified as spam or legitimate — useful for understanding the filter's decision-making and for troubleshooting unexpected classifications.</p>
